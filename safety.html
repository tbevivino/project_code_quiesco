Safety


Quiesco is composed of sucrose (or sugar) that has been formulated to be released slowly over the course of the day. It was made clear to the study participants that the "active" ingredient" of Quiesco would be the same as the placebo and that since this "active" ingredient was sugar they should not anticipate any side effects.

Indication

Quiesco™ (sucrose pom) is not a real product, but if it was, it would be indicated for the treatment of hypochondrisis (or hypochondria)in healthy individuals.

Important Safety Information

Your doctor should perform a complete health evaluation prior to prescribing Quiesco. You should not use Quiesco for any real medical problems.
The most common side effects of Quiesco were in line with those expected from a placebo sugar pill and were generally mild to moderate. Discontinue use if any side effect becomes bothersome.
Do not use Quiesco if you are allergic to sucrose or any other of the product's ingredients.
Talk to your doctor about any new symptoms that emerge while taking Quiesco as these could be a sign of a medical condition.
